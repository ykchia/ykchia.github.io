Short Bio

I received my PhD and MSc. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University in 2012, and my M.Eng degree (first class honors) from Imperial College, London. Since 2012, I have been working as a research scientist at the Institute for Infocomm Research, Singapore. I was a recipient of the Stanford Graduate Fellowship and the Agency for Science, Technology and Research Singapore National Science Scholarship for my graduate studies at Stanford University. I received the Singapore Public Service Commission Scholarship for my undergraduate studies at Imperial College, London.

My C.V. can be downloaded from my resume page.
